#Emotionalyze Help!

Welcome to #Emotionalyze! #Emotionalyze is designed to give you real-time information about how the country is feeling about a place, person, issue, or just about any other topic you can think to search. Simply type a search term in the box, press enter, and watch as #Emotionalyze helps you gain insight on a world of emotion! Remember: search terms must be five words or less. The more relevant the search term, the better the results.
